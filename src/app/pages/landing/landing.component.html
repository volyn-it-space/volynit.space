FAQ

Questions Volyn business owners usually ask.

What do I pay for? +

You pay for connecting your business to a ready website, launch setup, and the agreed support format.

What is included in monthly support? +

Monthly support can include hosting, maintenance, monitoring, small fixes, and bug fixing within the agreement.

What counts as a bug? +

A bug is something that does not work as originally agreed in the launched website or supported feature.

What counts as a paid change? +

A paid change is a new feature, new page, larger content change, design change, or request outside the original scope.

Can I take the website later? +

Yes. Ownership transfer can be arranged so the project can move to your own account or another support team.

Do you support multiple languages? +

Yes. Multi-language pages can be included when your customers need English plus another language.

Can I update content? +

Yes. An admin panel or simpler content editing flow can be included when your team needs direct updates.

How quickly can we launch? +

Launch speed depends on content, domain access, and the support format, but the ready-made model is designed to move quickly.
